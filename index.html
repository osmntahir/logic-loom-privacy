Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Logic Loom ("we", "our", "the App") is a mobile puzzle game platform developed and published by Logic Loom. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

By using Logic Loom, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal contact information.
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel).
  • We do not store any data on remote servers. All your game progress is stored locally on your device.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically by Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

Service Purpose Data Collected Policy
Google AdMob Display ads (banner, interstitial, rewarded) Advertising ID, device info, IP address, ad interaction data Google Privacy Policy
Google Play Billing In-app purchases & subscriptions Purchase history (managed by Google) Google Privacy Policy

2.3 Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data only on your device:

  • Game progress, puzzle completion status, and statistics
  • App settings and preferences (theme, locale, notification settings)
  • Hint credit balance and entitlement status
  • Ad impression history (used to limit ad frequency)

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all local data.

3. Advertising

Logic Loom displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable tracking for Logic Loom

You can remove all ads by purchasing the "No Ads Forever" option or subscribing to Logic Loom Pass within the App.

4. In-App Purchases

Logic Loom offers optional in-app purchases including subscriptions, one-time purchases, and consumable credit packs. All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment information (credit card numbers, bank details, etc.).

5. Children's Privacy

Logic Loom is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App contains advertisements that may not be suitable for children under 13.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on remote servers, there is no risk of a server-side data breach from our end. All user data remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security measures.

7. Notifications

The App may send local notifications (e.g., daily puzzle reminders) if you grant notification permission. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — we do not use push notification services or remote messaging. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings or within the App.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the App
  • Disable notifications from your device settings
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date". Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
